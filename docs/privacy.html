Privacy Policy

This policy describes how Hairmap handles customer, stylist, salon, booking, messaging, and uploaded media data.

Home Terms Support

Information We Collect

Hairmap may collect account information such as name, email address, display nickname, role, and authentication identifiers. When you create or manage a booking, we may collect appointment details including stylist, salon, selected service, date, time, client name, and phone number.

When you use community or profile features, Hairmap may collect user-generated content such as messages, comments, reviews, uploaded photos or videos, stylist portfolios, salon portfolios, service descriptions, and profile details.

How We Use Information

We use information to provide app functionality, including account login, salon and stylist discovery, booking management, stylist dashboards, chat, reviews, inspiration posts, uploads, moderation, support, and platform administration.

Data Storage And Service Providers

Hairmap uses Supabase for authentication, database storage, and media storage. Data may be processed by Supabase infrastructure for app functionality. If additional analytics, crash reporting, payments, or marketing tools are added in the future, this policy and App Store privacy disclosures should be updated before release.

Public Content

Stylist profiles, salon profiles, services, portfolio photos, ratings, reviews, approved inspiration posts, and public display names may be visible to other users. Do not upload content that you do not have permission to share.

Messages And Bookings

Customer-stylist messages and appointment details are used to coordinate salon services and platform support. Platform administrators may access records where needed for safety, troubleshooting, moderation, or customer support.

Your Choices

You may update profile information in the app where supported. You may request help with account, booking, content, deletion, or correction issues through the support page.

Children

Hairmap is not designed for children. Users should meet the minimum age requirements of their local jurisdiction and the App Store age rating.

Changes

We may update this policy as Hairmap changes. The latest version will be posted on this page.

Contact

For privacy or support questions, use the contact method listed on the Support page.
