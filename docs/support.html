What To Include

Booking Issues

If an appointment was created incorrectly, include the stylist name, salon name, selected service, date, time, and the email or nickname used in the app.

Stylist Or Salon Profile Issues

If you are a stylist or salon owner and need to update or remove public information, include your display name, salon name, district, and the fields that need correction.

Account And Data Requests

If you need help updating, correcting, or deleting account or public profile information, contact us by email and include the account email or display name used in Hairmap.
